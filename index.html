SpaPE: Spatial Positional Encodings for Spatially Aware Video Generation

SpaPE achieves higher camera pose accuracy than relevant baselines, including token-level camera-pose-conditioned video generation approaches, while maintaining the high image quality of the pretrained base model. In the following, we demonstrate the generated videos of our SpaPE compared with the baseline methods for both normal and reverse camera trajectories.
